Who We Are?

AnthropicBots E-Commerce is a modern open-source ecommerce platform focused on delivering an engaging online shopping experience with clean UI, scalable architecture, and contributor-friendly development practices.

Our goal is to create a real-world ecommerce ecosystem where developers can learn, contribute, and build advanced features including authentication systems, admin dashboards, AI-powered recommendations, payment integrations, and more.

Open-source development empowers innovation, collaboration, and scalable learning opportunities for contributors worldwide.
